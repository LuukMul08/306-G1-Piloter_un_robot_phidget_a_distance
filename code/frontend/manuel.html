Key Bindings

  • B

    Emergency Stop

    Instantly cuts power to the engine. Hold to reset.

  • Y

    Shift Gear Up

    Increase transmission gear for higher speeds.

  • A

    Shift Gear Down

    Decrease transmission gear for torque.

  • L

    Left Stick

    Move Forward / Backward (Fine control).

  • R

    Right Stick

    Look Left / Look Right.

Linear Acceleration

The triggers provide analog control. Pressing lightly moves the vehicle slowly, while a full press engages maximum power.

Right Trigger RT
Advance

Main throttle. Controls forward acceleration.

Left Trigger LT
Recede

Reverse throttle. Controls backward movement.
